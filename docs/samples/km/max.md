# Max — Billing Knowledge

## Invoice timing
Invoices are issued on the 1st of each month and are due within 15 days.

## Payment methods
We accept bank transfer and major credit cards. Card payments post within one business day.

## Late fees
A 1.5% monthly late fee applies after 30 days past due.

## Refund policy
Refunds for duplicate charges are reviewed within 5 business days. Provide the invoice number when calling.
