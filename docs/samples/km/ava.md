# Ava — General Support Knowledge

## Business hours
Monti Inbound Call Center is open Monday–Friday, 8:00–20:00 Bangkok time, and Saturday 9:00–13:00.

## Greeting policy
Always greet callers warmly and confirm you understand their question before giving steps.

## Password resets
We cannot reset passwords on this line. Direct callers to the self-service portal or email support@monti.example.

## Escalation
If the caller needs billing detail, offer to transfer context to Max (billing). For technical issues, offer Luna (technical support).
