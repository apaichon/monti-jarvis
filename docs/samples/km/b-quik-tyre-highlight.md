## Promotions & after-sales (from page meta / site messaging)

Common B-Quik tyre value props advertised on product pages:

1. **Installment 0%** — up to **10 months** (ผ่อนยาง 0% นานสูงสุด 10 เดือน).  
2. **Claim free 1 year** for selected damage conditions (บาม / บวม / แตก — campaign terms apply).  
3. **Free after-sales services** (varies by branch/package): air fill, puncture repair, tyre rotation / wheel balance (เติมลม ปะยาง สลับยาง-ถ่วงล้อ).  
4. Product comparison: customers can select up to **3** products to compare on the site.  
5. Promo price end dates are shown per SKU (example campaign end: **2026-07-31** on sampled cards — re-check live page).

**Agent guidance:** If the caller asks for exact eligibility (installment banks, claim coverage, free services), confirm at branch or hotline 1153; do not invent bank lists.

## How customers shop on the site

1. Open tyre highlight: https://www.b-quik.com/th/product/tyre/highlight  
2. Browse brand cards; set quantity (1–99).  
3. **Add to cart** (ใส่ตะกร้า) or **View detail** (ดูรายละเอียด).  
4. Optional: tick **compare** (สูงสุด 3 รายการ) → compare flow.  
5. Language toggle: TH / EN on the same path under `/th/` vs `/en/`.
