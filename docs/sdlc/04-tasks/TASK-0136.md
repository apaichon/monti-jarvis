# TASK-0136: Platform call-center analytics projection and aggregate contract

## Outcome

Define the ClickHouse query/projection contract that powers platform-wide and per-tenant call-center aggregates without duplicating Sprint 25 usage semantics.

## Acceptance criteria

1. Aggregate queries reuse the completed-call fact definition and inclusive date boundaries from Sprint 25.
2. The contract covers overall totals, tenant rows, channel/avatar/satisfaction breakdowns, package usage, and freshness.
3. Queries remain aggregate-only and do not select transcripts, customer identifiers, ticket notes, or audio paths.
4. ClickHouse unavailable/stale states are distinguishable from a valid empty range.

## Dependencies

- [SPRINT-025](../03-sprints/SPRINT-025.md) tenant analytics projection and metrics.
- [SPRINT-029](../03-sprints/SPRINT-029.md) platform monitoring freshness and unavailable-state contract.
